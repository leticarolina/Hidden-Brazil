Fernando de Noronha

The island of Fernando de Noronha is located in Pernambuco, in the Northeast region of Brazil.
Since it's an island, to get to Noronha you need to take a direct flight departing from Recife or Natal (bigger cities in the north of Brazil). The flight from Brazil mainland will take about an hour. Also, it is very important to mention that the island has two seasons: the dry season, which runs from August to February, and the rainy season, which runs from March to July.
